Our Story.

Established in 2013, Salt Cafe was born from a simple desire: to bring world-class wood-fired pizza and artisan coffee to the Dolphin Coast.

Positioned directly opposite Shaka's Rock Beach, we wanted to create a space that reflects the effortless luxury of coastal living. No pretense. Just premium ingredients, crafted by experts.

The Mechanics of the Bake

Thermal dynamics dictate the perfect slice. We push our ovens to the absolute limit, creating a micro-environment where the dough doesn't just cook—it reacts. The hydration flashes into steam, forcing the crust to rise and blister in seconds.

It requires constant calibration. Fire management is an active pursuit, demanding intuition and timing. This is where culinary art meets thermodynamic reality.

Calibration is Absolute

A great pour isn't accidental. It’s the result of rigorously managing variables: grind size, dose weight, water temperature, and tamp pressure. We zero our equipment daily to ensure the extraction hits the optimal ratio.

Whether it's a post-ride flat white or a pre-surf double shot, the output must be consistent, potent, and flawlessly textured.
